## 1: Death Becomes Her ##

Love was in the air as she looked out the window over the towering spires.  On a cot by her bed lay an ailing man.  

Suddenly the *vizier*, a noble grown old in the royal court, burst into her chamber. She shrieked in dismay.

_*SORAYA*_
How dare you! _(she exclaimed.)_  What right have you to burst into my chamber?!

_*VIZIER*_
No right but only need, Devi, _(answered the *vizier*.)_  This poison--

_*SORAYA*_
I tell you it is not poison! _(she cried.)_  It is  magic--

She ceased as the king spoke... his voice rasped as from far away.

_*KING*_
Soraya!  I am failing!

_*SORAYA*_
Brother! _(cried Soraya, catching his limp hand in a convulsive grasp.)_  I am here!  Do you not know me--

Her voice died at the utter vacancy of his face.
