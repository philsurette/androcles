1 Death Becomes Her

Love was in the air as she looked out the window over the towering spires.  On a cot by her bed lay an ailing man.  

Suddenly the _vizier_, a noble grown old in the royal court, burst into her chamber. She shrieked in dismay.

'How dare you!' she exclaimed.  'What right have you to burst into my chamber?!'

'No right but only need, Devi,' answered the *vizier*.  'This poison--'

'I tell you it is not poison!'she cried.  'It is  magic--'

She ceased as the king spoke... his voice rasped as from far away.

'Soraya!  I am failing!'

'Brother!' cried Soraya, catching his limp hand in a convulsive grasp.  'I am here!  Do you not know me--'

Her voice died at the utter vacancy of his face.
