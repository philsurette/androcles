# Ranked Project Gutenberg Plays by Cultural Mindshare

## Ranking method

I ranked only dramatic works for which I confirmed a full English text on Project Gutenberg, either as a standalone ebook or clearly inside an English-language collection. Scores are **comparative mindshare scores**, not invented traffic numbers: they synthesise direct-recognition proxies such as a dedicated play article and educational footprint, cultural footprint such as famous characters, quotations, or adaptation chains, and publication footprint such as Project Gutenberg shelf presence or visible 30-day download counts. Where exact Wikipedia pageview totals were not practical to extract reliably at scale from the public interface during this pass, I used article existence/depth plus study-guide and adaptation footprint as the more comparable proxy.
