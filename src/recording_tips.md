# Voice Performance Quick Reference

## Male Voice
- Lightly tighten your belly for steady breath support.  
- Keep vibration low in the chest.  
- Speak closer to the mic (3–6 inches), slightly off to the side.  
- Use clear, firm vowel sounds (avoid breathy starts).  
- Keep the tone grounded and confident.

### audacity
High-pass filter
- freq: 80hz
- roll-off: 24db

Compressor
- Threshold: -16
- Makeu-up gain: 0
- knee width: 20db
- ratio: 2
- lookahead: 1ms
- attack: 5ms
- release: 75ms

## Female Voice
- Relax your belly completely; no abdominal engagement.  
- Shift vibration forward into the face/mask area.  
- Add a touch of breathiness at the start of vowels.  
- Speak a little farther from the mic (6–10 inches).  
- Keep the tone lighter and smoother.  
- For shrewish characters: quicker pace, sharper consonants.  
- For dreamy characters: longer vowels, gentler rhythm.

### audacity
High-pass filter
- freq: 110hz
- roll-off: 24db

Filter Curve EQ:
- -3db at 300 Hz
- +3db at 6000 Hz

Compressor
- Threshold: -12
- Makeu-up gain: 0
- knee width: 30db
- ratio: 2
- lookahead: 1ms
- attack: 5ms
- release: 75ms
